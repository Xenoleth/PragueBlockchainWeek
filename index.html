About Prague Blockchain Week


Blockchain Week initiatives are happening all over the world whenever there is a large gathering of blockchain and cryptocurrencies enthusiasts around. The Prague Blockchain Week+ is no different, it's just a bit longer. This is an voluntary initiative which was formed around the Ethereum Devcon 4 and which will provide an overview and support for all the blockchain technology related events happening in Prague between Oct 24. and Nov 4. 2018.

Feel free to reach out root@polynom.com if you have any questions.

Venues of Prague Blockchain Week

Prague Congress Center

Large neofunctionalist building built in 1981 which is currently the largest congress center venue in Czech Republic.

Paralelni Polis

The unique crypto-only hacker base which is facilitating a discussion platform for topics related to tech, art and science.

National House

Early 20th century building which served as a central point of a former town Smichov (nowadays one of the central districts of Prague).

Vnitroblock

Former factory hall and bunch of garages placed in a backyard of residential buildings recently remaded as a hip cultural center.

Ackee HQ

The top floor of Danube House is where Czech development studio Ackee has its HQ and hosts many public events.

Node 5

One of the Prague's booming startup hubs, which servers as a workspace, event venue.

Kasarna Karlin

Old unused barracks building remade as a peaceful community center.

Opero

Brand new co-working center in the historical hearth of Prague.
